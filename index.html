Rosella Estate is an American-style Victorian mansion. It is set surrounded by magnificent gardens that will allow you to enjoy a relaxing stay. Lovingly restored, the estate offers you the opportunity for a luxurious weekend getaway, or to enjoy our relaxing home with our budget rooms. The house itself is set amongst the picturesque Adelaide hills. This region is renowned for its fine dining and for producing some of the best red wines in Australia.
There now is an opportunity to stay whether for a short stay or little while longer. Rosella Estate BnB offers accomodation for everyone. The estate offers both luxury and budget accommodation, panoramic views or private courtyards. With the finest of linen to match the elegance of the estate you can expect comfort and luxury. A seasonal breakfast prepared by our in house staff will greet you, with local wine tasting tours also on offer, come and be swept away by the magic of the Adelaide Hills.

Luxury Accommodation

  • The Penthouse with amazing panoramic view, private bathroom with spa bath. His and her robes to wear with the fluffist of towels. Although part of this mansion, it still provides that honeymoon privacy.

  • Imagine opening the door to your suite to see what luxury really looks like; four poster beds, quality linen and tasteful furnishings. Well these rooms are for you then. With private courtyards, water features, and luxury day beds that will make for that perfect country escape. Each suite is equipped with their own stunning bathroom that exudes quality and comfort.

Budget Accommodation

  • Located on either the ground or first floor, our beautiful rooms take in the spectacular views. Although there is a shared bathroom, these rooms are still highly sought after. The garden access room located on the ground floor allows for you to come and go with out disturbing the others. These rooms have been designed with comfort in mind and all at an affordable price.

Whatever room you are after you will still have full access to all the facilities including; the swimming pool, tennis courts, wi-fi, and common rooms located on the ground floor.
Wine and food tours of the local vineyards can also be arranged.
